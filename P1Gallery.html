Why Their Garden?

The mission of this website is to honor the fallen women who have bravely served our country by sharing their stories, raising awareness, and preserving their legacy. These women, who made the ultimate sacrifice, deserve to be remembered not only for their courage but for their humanity and the values they stood for. Through this platform, I seek to inspire others by shedding light on their lives, ensuring their contributions are never forgotten. In a world where misinformation and false claims about our country persist, it is vital to celebrate the real heroes who defended our freedoms. This website serves as a tribute to their unwavering spirit and a reminder of the profound impact their service has had on our nation.
