In Loving Memory of Eden Nimri

Biography

Eden Nimri was a bright and determined young woman from Kfar Saba, known for her strong leadership skills, compassion, and deep love for her country. Growing up in a close-knit family, Eden excelled academically and socially, always motivated by a desire to make a meaningful impact. After high school, she enlisted in the IDF and served in the elite Oketz Unit, specializing in working with military dogs. Her dedication to her role, along with her courage and perseverance, earned her the admiration of her fellow soldiers and commanders. Eden was known for her calm under pressure and her ability to lead others through challenging situations. Tragically, Eden was killed in the Hamas attack on October 7, 2023, while bravely defending her base near the Gaza border. Her loss was deeply felt by her community, family, and fellow soldiers. Eden's legacy of strength, kindness, and dedication to her country lives on, with various memorial initiatives established to honor her memory and her contributions to the safety and security of Israel.
