In Loving Memory of Roni Eshel

Biography

Roni Eshel was a courageous and compassionate young woman from Ashkelon, deeply committed to serving her country and helping others. Growing up by the sea, she was known for her leadership, empathy, and strong sense of responsibility. After excelling in school, Roni chose to enlist in the IDF's elite Search and Rescue Unit, where she took on the demanding role of saving lives during emergencies and natural disasters, both within Israel and in international missions. Her dedication, humility, and determination earned her the respect and admiration of her peers and commanders, who often described her as a role model of resilience and commitment. Roni’s service was defined by her unwavering willingness to put others before herself, always standing on the frontlines when it mattered most. On October 7, 2023, during the Hamas attack on her base near the Gaza border, Roni was killed while heroically defending her comrades. Her legacy of bravery, kindness, and service continues to inspire, and numerous memorial initiatives have been established to honor her life and the values she embodied.
